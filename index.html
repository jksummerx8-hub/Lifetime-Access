Service Description

更換裝置或重新安裝？ / Changing Devices?

本產品權限與您的 Apple ID 永久綁定。若您更換裝置或重新安裝 App，只需登入同一個 Apple ID 並點擊「恢復購買」即可找回功能。

This permission is permanently linked to your Apple ID. If you change your device or reinstall the app, simply sign in with the same Apple ID and tap "Restore Purchase" to regain all features.

一次購買，終身通行 / Lifetime Access

選擇「終身版」，您的權限將永久記錄在 Apple 雲端。無論未來更換哪一代 iPhone，您的專屬功能都將無縫回歸。

By choosing the "Lifetime" plan, your permissions are permanently recorded in Apple's cloud. No matter which iPhone you upgrade to, your exclusive features will seamlessly return.

Q：換手機需要重新付費嗎？
Will I need to pay again if I change phones? A：完全不需要。 / Absolutely not.
帳號綁定 / Account Linkage 購買紀錄與您的 Apple ID 連結，儲存於 Apple 加密伺服器。
Your purchase record is linked to your Apple ID and stored on Apple's encrypted servers.
跨裝置使用 / Cross-Device Use 您可以在所有登入相同 Apple ID 的 iOS 裝置上共用此權限。
You can share this permission on all iOS devices logged in with the same Apple ID.
操作簡單 / Easy to Use 在新手機點擊 「恢復購買 (Restore Purchase)」，系統驗證後即可自動解鎖。
On your new phone, tap "Restore Purchase" on the purchase page. The features will unlock automatically after verification.
